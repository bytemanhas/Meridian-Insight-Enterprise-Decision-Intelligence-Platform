# 📊 Meridian Insight™ – Enterprise Decision Intelligence Platform (EDIP)

## Overview

Meridian Insight™ is an enterprise-grade Business Intelligence and Decision Support platform designed to provide executives with a 360° view of organizational performance across Sales, Finance, Marketing, Inventory, Operations, and Customer Analytics.

The project demonstrates an end-to-end Business Intelligence workflow—from business requirement documentation and synthetic data generation to data transformation, analytics, and interactive Power BI dashboards.

---

## Business Objective

To develop a centralized Executive Decision Intelligence Platform that enables stakeholders to:

- Monitor organizational KPIs
- Analyze business performance
- Identify trends and opportunities
- Improve operational efficiency
- Support strategic decision-making through interactive dashboards
